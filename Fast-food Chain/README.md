# 🍔 Fast-Food Operational Excellence & Growth (BI Analytics Project)

## 📌 Project Overview
As a **Business Intelligence (BI) Consultant**, this project focuses on delivering a **data-driven strategy** to improve **net profit** and **customer satisfaction** for a regional fast-food chain.

Using a dataset of **1,000 transactions**, this analysis combines **SQL, spreadsheets, and Power BI** to uncover actionable insights and support executive decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Improve **store performance and profitability**
- Increase **customer satisfaction and retention**
- Identify **operational inefficiencies**
- Optimize **marketing and promotion strategies**
- Strengthen **food safety and quality compliance**

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **SQL** – Data cleaning, transformation, and querying  
- **Microsoft Excel / Google Sheets** – Data preprocessing & validation  
- **Power BI** – Data visualization & dashboard creation  

---

## 🔄 Data Cleaning & ETL
Key data preparation steps included:

- Converted `OrderDate` into proper **datetime format**
- Split **Purchase Date** into:
  - Year, Month, Day
- Combined `Store ID` + `Store Location` → Created **Store Name**
- Verified **Net Profit accuracy** across all records
- Removed duplicates and handled missing values
- Ensured data consistency for analysis

---

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### 🏪 Store Performance
- Identified **highest and lowest performing stores** by total revenue
- Counted total number of unique stores
- Highlighted performance gaps across locations

### ❌ Order Cancellation Analysis
- Measured **cancellation rates per store**
- Identified operational bottlenecks (e.g., delays, service issues)

---

## 👥 Customer Insights

### 📢 Marketing Channel Effectiveness
- Analyzed **Average Order Value (AOV)** by channel:
  - Social Media
  - Email
  - TV
- Identified the **highest-performing channel** for revenue generation

### 🎖️ Loyalty Program Analysis
- Evaluated relationship between **Customer Loyalty Tier** and spending
- Assessed effectiveness of loyalty incentives

---

## 🧪 Quality & Safety Analysis
- Analyzed correlation between:
  - **Health Inspection Score**
  - **Customer Feedback Score**
- Identified that lower inspection scores often align with lower customer satisfaction

---

## 📊 Dashboard (Power BI)
An interactive dashboard was developed to visualize:

- Revenue by Store Location  
- Cancellation Rates by Store  
- AOV by Marketing Channel  
- Customer Loyalty Tier Performance  
- Health Inspection vs Customer Feedback (Correlation)  

---

## 🚀 Key Recommendations

### 1. Store Performance & Quality Control
- Targeted Improvement: Prioritize stores with both low Health Inspection scores and low Customer Feedback ratings.
- Root Cause Analysis: Conduct targeted surveys at these locations to identify the primary drivers of low customer satisfaction.
- Staff Development: Implement training programs focused on customer service excellence and optimized cooking times to ensure food quality.
- Compliance & Infrastructure: Increase food safety monitoring and evaluate underperforming stores for potential remodeling.

---

### 2. Marketing & Promotions Strategy
- Ad Optimization: Partner with the marketing team to analyze advertising timeframes and maximize campaign effectiveness.
- Strategic Couponing: Discontinue coupon validity during high-demand periods (the 1st through the 10th of the month) to protect margins.
- Dynamic Promotions: Rotate between "Happy Hour," "Deal of the Day," and general promotions to maintain customer interest through variety.

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Significant **performance variation across store locations**
- Strong relationship between **food safety and customer satisfaction**
- Certain marketing channels drive **higher customer spending**
- Data-driven strategies can directly improve **profitability and retention**
